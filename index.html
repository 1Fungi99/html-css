Welcome to Dichotamy Studios

As a child, I often dreamt of being an astronaut; flying through the stars and exploring the final frontier. This aspiration manifested in several ways: with LEGOs, for example, I always favored the Star Wars sets. I would imagine what might be out there, and I'm certain that my eyes, full of curiosity and the imagination of a child, glistened with the light of the stars themselves. My dream of being an astronaut seeded a deep-seated love for computers. Now, as a college student, that love has blossomed to encompass the joys of software engineering. Through coding at Collin Community College, the University of Texas at Arlington, and currently at Southern Methodist University, I have broadened myself. As a quick learner, I excel at applying new skills and concepts, expanding my repertoire of programming languages, and refining my talents with responsive website design and development. To date, I have worked extensively with Javascript, HTML, CSS, Git/Github, Bootstrap, jQuery, Matlab, MySQL, NodeJS, React, Python, and C#. I have experience with C++ and will soon be learning Java. I eagerly look forward to continuing to grow and learn, working with new programming languages and frameworks as well as other opportunities through-which I might improve myself.
